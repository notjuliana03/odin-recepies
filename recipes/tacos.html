Taquitos de carnita:p

A typical mexican dish made with slow pulled pork in a corn tortilla.

Ingredients

  • Pork shoulder
  • Spices
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Jalapeño
  • Orange juice
  • Corn tortillas
  • Cilantro
  • Lime juice

Instructions

  1. Mix in a bowl oregano, cumin, black pepper, salt and chilli powder.
  2. Rub and season the pork meat with the spices and place it in a slow cooker.
  3. Add onions, minced garlic, jalapeños and orange juice to the slow cooker.
  4. Cook the meat for 8-10 hours in low heat or 5-6 hours in high heat (until the meat falls apart)
  5. Shred the pork
  6. Warm the corn tortillas in a pan
  7. Add the shredded pork
  8. Add toping to taste (onion, cilantro, lime juice, cheese, etc)

Enjoy :)
